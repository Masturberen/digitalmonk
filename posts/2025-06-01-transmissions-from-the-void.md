Every signal starts as noise. This site is my attempt to turn the static into something with shape. Welcome to the new version of Digital Monk.

## Why build this?

The modern web has become a place of templates, trackers, and engagement loops. I wanted something different — a personal corner of the internet that feels *mine*.

This OS-style interface is an experiment. A blog wrapped in a retro desktop metaphor. Games embedded like apps. Music as mixtapes. Photos as files.

## What to expect

Posts about music, code, games, and whatever else passes through the void. No schedule. No algorithm. Just transmissions.

> "The street finds its own uses for things." — William Gibson
