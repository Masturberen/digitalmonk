Found three gems in a tiny shop off Waterlooplein last Tuesday. The kind of shop where the owner eyes you suspiciously until you pull out the right record, then suddenly becomes your best friend.

## The Finds

**Ryuichi Sakamoto — 千のナイフ (1978)**
First press. Some wear on the sleeve but the vinyl is near mint. That opening synth sequence sounds completely different on wax.

**Yellow Magic Orchestra — Unknown B-side**
Still figuring out exactly which release this is from. The A-side label is hand-written. It could be a promo.

**Mystery Record**
Thin cardboard sleeve, no label, no markings. Sounds like city pop but with something off about the tuning. Haunting.

## The Shop

No name on the door. Just a small handwritten sign: *platen*. Two rooms, floor to ceiling. The owner only accepts cash and speaks exclusively in Dutch and Japanese, which worked out fine.

I'll be back next month.
